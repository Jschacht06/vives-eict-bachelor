# Making a Sandwich

Describe the process of making a ham and cheese sandwich. Now imagine you had to explain it to a computer. Have you forgotten anything that would be obvious for us humans?

## Solution

First go to the storage and take 2 slices of bread
Go to the kitchen and put the 2 slices of bread on the table
Go the the fridge and search for 1 package of ham and 1 package of cheese
Then take 1 slice of ham and 1 slice of cheese out of the packages
Go back to the table and take 1 slice of bread
Lay it flat on the table and then put the ham and cheese on top of the bread
Take the other slice of bread and put it on top of the ham and cheese
